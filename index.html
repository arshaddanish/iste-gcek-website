    About Us

    ISTE is all about teamwork, as a team we work towards in building a society that benefits the students who take their first step in technical industry.

    About ISTE GCEK

    The ISTE Student Chapter of Govt. College of Engineering Kannur was re-established in the academic year 2016-17 to provide a common platform for students to exhibit and cultivate their talents which help in their all-round career development.

    Mission

    National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System.

    Vision

    • Quality training programmes for the teachers and administrators of technical institutions to update their knowledge and skills in their fields of activity.

    • Assist and contribute in the production and development of top quality professional engineers and technicians needed by the industry and other organisations.

    • Providing guidance and training to students to develop better learning skills and personality.

    Our Activities

    Here are some of the activities we have conducted. It was very beneficial for students as they were provided with opportunities to develop their desired skills.

    Gate Coaching

    An initiative by students to support other students in studies and cracking the GATE Examination under Club Contrive. Faculties also contribute their support.

    Workshop Series

    Workshops from various professionals are provided on demand. The topics are chosen very wisely prioritising the demands of our fellow members.

    Project Building

    It is a space where students who are interested in taking up some projects can confidently come with/without a basic notion. It is handled by Think-Tank Forum.

    Alumni Connect

    A talk series in association with the Bangalore Alumni Association of GCEK (CEAAK). Here, different technical topics are discussed once a month.

    Soft Skill Development

    Our soft skill development programs are focused on making students confident for facing the industry tomorrow.

    Branch Specific Events

    Programs are held related to core fields that aims at curbing the academic and industrial gap.

    See our Events

    We conducted a lot of events to explore the talent of every student and they were able to showcase their ability on this platform.
